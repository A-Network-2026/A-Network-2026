ANTS Program | View-Only Community Language

Use ant language for community identity, not reward-program language.

The ANTS Program is the public naming layer for colony participation in A Network. It gives members a clear ant-language identity such as Worker Ants, Builder Ants, Scouts, Soldiers, Farmers, Nurses, Queen Ant, and Swarm Ants without mixing that identity into Web2 mining rewards, ANTS accounting, or ANET settlement rules.

Why ANTS Program

"Reward program" language is too narrow and too easy to confuse with payout promises. "ANTS Program" matches the actual design better: ant-language identity, community coordination, transparent growth, and future Web5 participation.

view-only identity community-first not mining rewards not on-chain payout

ANET-Chain Transparency

Members can inspect public blockchain activity through ANET-Chain. That chain view is for transparency, transfer visibility, and settlement observation. It does not convert the ANTS Program into an on-chain reward ledger.

Language Rules

The terminology matters because the project now enforces separate economic layers.

Use ANTS Program

Use ant-language labels for colony visibility, social structure, and future community participation.

Do Not Say Reward Program

That phrase implies fixed payouts or earnings logic. The current colony model is view-only and separate from mining rewards.

Keep Layers Separate

Web2 mining, Web3 visibility, and Web5 community coordination each have their own purpose and should not be conflated in user-facing language.

Ant Roles

These are identity and coordination roles for the ANTS Program and future ANET Core participation language.

Worker Ants

Everyday builders of the colony through consistent participation.

Builder Ants

Infrastructure, design, tooling, and product contributors.

Scout Ants

Discovery, outreach, route finding, and ecosystem expansion.

Soldier Ants

Security-minded members focused on defense and resilience.

Farmer Ants

Long-term value gardeners who cultivate sustainable growth.

Nurse Ants

Onboarding, care, support, continuity, and education.

Queen Ant

A symbolic continuity role in the program language, not a centralized controller.

Swarm Ants

Collective movement during launches, migrations, and major community actions.

The 3 Economic Layers

The ANTS Program should reinforce this model, not blur it.

Web2 Economy

Ant Work sessions, ANTS accounting, mined ANET ledger rules, anti-abuse checks, and app-native source-of-truth balances.

Web3 Economy

Public BNB Chain visibility, contract transparency, future partner entry, and future buy-side references for broader ecosystem participation.

Web5 Economy

Colony coordination, ANTS Program identity, future ANET Core participation, and community structure that connects the broader Web2 and Web4 / ANET-Chain direction.

Current Rule

The ANTS Program is currently view-only. Colony points, ranks, snapshots, and any future partner rewards must remain separate from Web2 mining rewards and separate from ANET / ANTS settlement.
