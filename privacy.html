Privacy Policy

A Network ("we", "our", or "us") operates the A Network application and related services. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and disclose information when users access our Web2, Web3, and Web4-related features.

A Network is a long-term ecosystem project. It is not a guaranteed-profit program, not financial advice, and not a get-rich-quick scheme.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

  • Email address and login credentials for account creation and authentication.
  • Device or session identifiers used to secure accounts and prevent abuse.
  • App activity and usage information used for functionality, reliability, and service improvement.
  • Wallet addresses or wallet-related identifiers when users access Web3 features.
  • Support or contact information when users communicate with us.

2. How We Use Information

We use information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, maintain, and secure the application.
  • Authenticate users and manage user sessions.
  • Support app features across off-chain Web2 services and on-chain Web3 integrations.
  • Improve functionality, performance, user experience, and fraud detection.
  • Comply with legal obligations and protect the integrity of the ecosystem.

3. Web2, Web3, and Web4 Context

A Network may include separate but linked product layers:

  • Web2 off-chain features, including app-native account systems and service logic.
  • Web3 on-chain features, including wallet displays and token-related blockchain information.
  • Web4 coordination concepts that connect Web2 and Web3 through governed product logic.

Not all app activity is blockchain activity. Some features are off-chain, some are on-chain, and some may connect both systems.
