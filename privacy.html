Privacy Policy

A Network LLC ("A Network", "we", "our", or "us") operates the A Network application and related services. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and disclose information when users access our Web2, Web3, and Web4-related features.

A Network is a long-term ecosystem project. It is not a guaranteed-profit program, not financial advice, and not a get-rich-quick scheme.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

  • Email address and login credentials for account creation, authentication, and account recovery.
  • Email verification metadata, including one-time password (OTP) code status and expiration timestamps.
  • Device, IP, and session identifiers used to secure accounts, detect abuse, and maintain service integrity.
  • Ant Work activity data, including session start/completion, elapsed time checks, and successful session counters.
  • Wallet addresses or wallet-related identifiers when users access Web3 features.
  • Support or contact information when users communicate with us.

2. How We Use Information

We use information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, maintain, and secure the application.
  • Authenticate users, verify email ownership with OTP flows, and manage sessions.
  • Operate Ant Work features, reward accounting, anti-abuse checks, and supply protection controls.
  • Support app features across off-chain Web2 services and on-chain Web3 integrations.
  • Improve functionality, performance, user experience, and fraud detection.
  • Comply with legal obligations and protect the integrity of the ecosystem.

3. Web2, Web3, and Web4 Context

A Network may include separate but linked product layers:

  • Web2 off-chain features, including app-native account systems and service logic.
  • Web3 on-chain features, including wallet displays and token-related blockchain information.
  • Web4 coordination concepts that connect Web2 and Web3 through governed product logic.

Not all app activity is blockchain activity. Some features are off-chain, some are on-chain, and some may connect both systems.

4. Advertising

We may use Google AdMob or related advertising services. These services may collect and process data according to their own policies and technologies, including advertising identifiers, app interaction signals, and device-level metadata used for ad delivery, measurement, and fraud prevention.

Learn more: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell users' personal information. We may share limited information with service providers, hosting providers, analytics providers, ad partners, email/OTP delivery providers, blockchain data providers, or legal authorities when reasonably necessary for service operation, security, compliance, or fraud prevention.

6. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information. However, no method of internet transmission or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Children's Privacy

This service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If we become aware that such data has been provided, we will take reasonable steps to remove it.

8. Data Retention

We retain information for as long as necessary to operate the service, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, maintain security, and comply with applicable law. Security-sensitive records such as OTP verification data, Ant Work session logs, and abuse-prevention signals may be retained for security and audit purposes.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updated version will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
