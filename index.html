Three Linked Economies

A clearer model helps users, builders, and researchers understand what is off-chain, what is on-chain, and how the long-term Web4 layer ties both together.

Web2 Economy

App-native services, engagement logic, growth systems, user operations, and off-chain utility. This layer focuses on usability, product access, and operational scale.

Web3 Economy

The decentralized center of A Network. Wallet ownership, transparent contract logic, and verifiable token balances live here and anchor ecosystem trust.

Web4 Economy

The coordination layer. Web4 connects Web2 utility with Web3 settlement under defined rules, compliance logic, and long-term ecosystem governance.
